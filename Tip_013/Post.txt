Need random points on a circle or sphere? There's a critical detail most developers miss.

Unity's Random.insideUnitCircle and Random.onUnitSphere handle the complex math of uniform distribution. But here's the gotcha: if you want points ON a circle's edge (not inside it), you MUST normalize before scaling. Otherwise you'll get points scattered throughout the interior.
