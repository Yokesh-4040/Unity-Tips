Unity's [ColorUsage] attribute is a game-changer for controlling color pickers in the Inspector!

You can enable HDR for emissive materials that need to glow, disable the alpha channel when transparency isn't needed, or combine both for advanced VFX. It's a small attribute that makes your inspector cleaner and prevents configuration errors.

I've documented practical examples here:
https://github.com/Yokesh-4040/Unity-Tips/blob/main/Tip_003/README.md
